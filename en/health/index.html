Discomfort and pain should be eliminated, not hidden

In most of those who complain of frequent back pain, the origin of this problem is almost the same: sedentary work, sedentary lifestyle, heavy bags on one shoulder. It is not surprising that back pain is becoming more frequent and makes you think about it. The absolute majority of people choose one way - pills and ointments. Pharmacists will gladly offer you a full range of “effective” means of getting rid of your problems. However, after some time you will realize that it has a temporary effect and this drives you to despair. We know many stories our clients have had to go through before they come to us at Body Factory. Here we understand you from half a word. At the initial consultation with our massage specialist, we determine the degree and severity of the current condition, what exactly causes muscle spasm, how to get rid of stagnant phenomena in the neck, and so with all the muscles of your body, the functionality of which we know perfectly. The first three sessions will be decisive, it is after them that you will feel that the discomfort begins to change its emphasis and already with the fourth session the problem will begin to disappear. Usually, the feeling that everything is behind comes already after the sixth session. You will feel that your movements have become free, nothing affects your sleep, and the morning is more energetic than ever.
