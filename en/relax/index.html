How to relieve the feeling of fatigue in 1 session and improve well-being?

Intense rhythm of life, irregular work schedule, physical and emotional devastation, loss of interest, apathy are the first symptoms of emotional exhaustion. Emotional exhaustion = loss of ability to work. Massage is an excellent medicine for any psycho-emotional illness. A relaxing massage perfectly relieves psychological and physical tension, calms the nervous system, helps to get rid of stress, restores the body as a whole. If your body and thoughts need a reboot, visit the Body Factory. In order to fully relax and enjoy the session, we recommend that you discuss all your wishes with our specialists before the start. In turn, specialists will announce the details of the service. All relaxing massages are performed only on the back surface of the body, so as not to disturb you by turning over and changing sensations. After the massage session, we recommend that you enjoy tea or your favorite drink and take your time, so that the body gradually recovers. We recommend attending massage sessions regularly every week. Just 1 hour of the session can relieve fatigue and tension from the muscles, reset the body and give productivity for the whole week. We have made sure that you have a wide choice of different types of relaxing massage.

  • Hot stones massage - necessary for fatigue, nervous tension and long-term mental stress. In order to ensure complete immersion in a state of rest, the specialist alternates hand movements and warm basalt stones. Such a change in sensations perfectly relieves tension.
  • Candle aroma massage is able to restore strength and relax the body to the fingertips. Warm melted oil warms and nourishes the skin well, expands pores, promotes rapid penetration of useful trace elements.
  • Aroma massage - a massage session that involves the use of essential oils. Essential oils, depending on their type, have a wide range of effects: improving immunity, relieving nervous tension, etc.
